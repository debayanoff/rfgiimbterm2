Nutrition

Diet Plans

Follow a curated diet chart or build your own custom meal plan.

Diet Chart for Weight Loss

The goal here is a caloric deficit — consuming fewer calories than you burn while prioritising protein and fibre to maintain muscle and stay full.

MealFood Options
Early Morning1 glass of warm water with lemon  or  1 cup of green tea.
BreakfastVegetable oats, moong dal chilla, or 2 boiled egg whites with a slice of whole-grain toast.
Mid-MorningA small bowl of papaya or an apple.
Lunch1 bowl of dal, 1 small bowl of sabzi (vegetables), a large salad, and 1–2 multigrain rotis.
Evening SnackA handful of roasted makhana (fox nuts) or 1 cup of buttermilk.
DinnerGrilled chicken / paneer with sautéed vegetables  or  a large bowl of clear vegetable soup with sprouts.

⚡ Key Tips

  • Hydration: Drink at least 2.5–3 litres of water daily.
  • Avoid: Processed sugars, sodas, and deep-fried snacks.
  • Portion Control: Use smaller plates to manage serving sizes visually.

Diet Chart for Weight Gain

The goal is a caloric surplus — consuming more calories than you burn, focusing on healthy fats, complex carbohydrates, and high protein to build lean mass.

MealFood Options
Early MorningA handful of soaked almonds and walnuts  or  a banana.
BreakfastStuffed parathas with curd, a large bowl of porridge with nuts, or an omelet with 2–3 eggs and cheese toast.
Mid-MorningA fruit smoothie with peanut butter  or  a glass of whole milk with protein powder.
Lunch1 bowl of thick dal, 1 bowl of rice, 2 rotis, a bowl of curd, and a portion of chicken / paneer / fish.
Evening SnackA bowl of boiled chickpeas (chana) or a peanut butter sandwich.
DinnerPaneer butter masala or soya chunks curry with 2–3 ghee-topped rotis and a side of salad.

⚡ Key Tips

  • Meal Frequency: Eat 5–6 smaller meals instead of 3 large ones to avoid feeling overly full.
  • Liquid Calories: Add healthy fats like olive oil, ghee, or nut butters to your meals.
  • Strength Training: Focus on lifting weights to ensure the weight gain is muscle rather than just body fat.
